Frequently Asked Questions

Why Microdermabrasion?

Microdermabrasion is an aesthetic procedure that involves removing dead skin cells from the surface of the skin using a mechanical exfoliation device. Moreover, it involves using a special machine that sprays fine crystals (Aluminum oxide) onto the skin, which removes the outermost layer of dead skin cells. Hence, the doctor can perform the procedure gently and non-invasively on the face, neck, chest, and hands.

What is Microdermabrasion?

Microdermabrasion can help to improve the appearance of fine lines, wrinkles, age spots, acne scars, and hyperpigmentation. It can also help to improve skin texture, tone, and overall appearance. The procedure is safe, painless, and requires no downtime. Doctors can perform microdermabrasion as a standalone treatment or in combination with other cosmetic procedures such as facials, chemical peels, and dermal fillers.
